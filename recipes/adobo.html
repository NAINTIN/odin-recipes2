Chicken Adobo Recipe Description:

Chicken Adobo is a really hard recipe to cook but if you follow these steps correctly you will be fine.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 pc onion sliced
  • 1 kilogram chicken cut ups
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 pcs bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns,slightly crushed
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar packed
  • Optional: 1 cup kale or spinach

Steps:

  1. Step 1 Heat oil in pan and saut√© garlic and onions. Then add chicken to the pan and sear on all sides, until you have a little browning in the chicken skin.
  2. Step 2: Pour in vinegar, soy sauce and water. Add bay leaves, pepper and Knorr Chicken Cubes. Bring to a boil over high heat then reduce heat to simmer, but do not cover the pan. Continue to simmer for 10 mins.
  3. Step 3: Remove chicken pieces from sauce and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
  4. Step 4: Put back fried chicken pieces into sauce. Add sugar and let simmer again for another 10 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Serve warm.
